About

We build infrastructure
that outlasts companies.

Hunt Information Systems is a founder-led firm at the intersection of cryptography, AI orchestration, and human legacy. We don't chase trends. We build what lasts.

Memory is the foundation of intelligence—human or artificial. But most memory systems are locked behind proprietary APIs, vulnerable to silent mutation, and impossible to audit. We think that's a structural failure, not a feature gap.

We design sovereign memory architectures: systems where every event is cryptographically chained, independently verifiable, and owned by the people who created it. No blockchain required. No buzzwords. Just Ed25519 signatures, SHA-256 hashes, and deterministic event sourcing.

Our clients range from AI startups building multi-agent systems to families preserving generational knowledge. The common thread: they need memory infrastructure they can trust without trusting us.
