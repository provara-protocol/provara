About

Hunt Information Systems LLC is a technology company focused on sovereign memory infrastructure. We believe that digital continuity should not depend on any single company surviving, any server staying online, or any platform deciding to keep your data.

Our approach is fundamentally different from cloud-based solutions: we build systems that are self-sovereign, cryptographically verifiable, and designed to outlive their creators.

Design Philosophy

SOVEREIGN

Your data lives in your files, not on our servers. You control the keys. You control the data.

VERIFIABLE

Cryptographic signatures and hash chains provide tamper-evidence. Trust through verification, not promises.

INHERITABLE

Systems designed for 50-year durability. Your digital legacy should outlive you.

OPEN

Apache 2.0 licensed. Specifications are public. Implementations are portable across languages.

Projects

Provara Protocol

provara.dev

Tamper-evident memory substrate for AI systems and long-lived digital institutions. Ed25519 signatures, SHA-256 hashing, event-sourced architecture. 232 tests passing. One external dependency. Apache 2.0 licensed.

Stack: Python 3.10+, cryptography≥41.0
Status: v1.0.0 Stable Release
Repository: github.com/provara-protocol/provara

Provara Family Vault

provara.app

Consumer-facing application for family memory preservation. Store documents, photos, and important records with cryptographic verification. Designed for non-technical users who want sovereign control over their family's digital legacy.

Status: Beta
Platform: Cross-platform (Windows, macOS, Linux)

MCP Server

GitHub

Model Context Protocol server enabling AI agents to write to Provara vaults. Any MCP-compatible AI assistant can maintain cryptographic memory without understanding the underlying protocol. 8 tools including append_event, verify_chain, generate_digest, and snapshot_state.

Transport: stdio + SSE (HTTP)
Tests: 22 passing
