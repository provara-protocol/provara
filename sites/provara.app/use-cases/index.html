Multi-Generational Heritage

Family photos and stories shouldn't just live on a social media platform that might not exist in 20 years. Provara allows you to create a cryptographically signed heritage vault that can be passed down to your grandchildren.

Every memory is timestamped and verified, ensuring your family history remains authentic and untampered.

Digital Estate & Inheritance

Ensure your loved ones have access to your digital life when it matters most. Securely store recovery phrases, account lists, and legal documents.

With Provara's inheritance logic, you can designate trusted actors who can unlock specific sections of your vault under conditions you define, without ever needing a central authority.

Verifiable AI Agent Memory

As AI agents become part of our daily lives, they need a memory layer that we can trust. Provara provides a tamper-evident event log for AI observations and decisions.

Verify that your AI assistant is operating within its policy boundaries and has a consistent, auditable history of its actions.
