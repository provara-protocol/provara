Security & Trust

Don't Trust Us.
Verify.

Provara Family Vault is built on the open-source Provara Protocol. Every claim we make about security is mathematically provable, publicly auditable, and independently verifiable.

Ed25519 Digital Signatures

Every record is signed with military-grade elliptic curve cryptography. Tampering is mathematically impossible without your keys.

SHA-256 Integrity Hashing

Content is hashed and chained. Change a single character anywhere, and the entire chain breaks. Your records stay authentic.

Key-Based Access

You hold the keys — literally. No passwords stored on servers, no master keys we can use. If we disappear, your vault remains yours.

No Cloud Dependency

Your data never touches our servers. No cloud required. Works fully offline on your own devices, your own terms.
