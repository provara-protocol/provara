How Provara Works

Provara isn't just another cloud storage app. It's a cryptographic memory protocol designed to protect your family's legacy for the next 50 years and beyond.

01

Cryptographic Identity

When you set up Provara, you generate a unique pair of cryptographic keys. These keys live only on your devices‚Äînever on our servers. These keys are your digital signature, allowing you to prove that every record in your vault was created by you and hasn't been modified since.

Unlike passwords, these keys cannot be "reset" by a third party. You are in total control of your identity.

  "status": "ACTIVE"

02

The Causal Chain

Every time you add a document, memory, or record, Provara creates an "Event". Each new event contains a cryptographic hash of the event that came before it. This creates a "Causal Chain".

If someone tries to change a record from five years ago, the hash of that record will change. This breaks the link to every subsequent record, making the tampering instantly visible. This is how we guarantee the integrity of your legacy.

03

Offline-First Storage

Your vault is a self-contained folder that lives on your own hardware‚Äîyour computer, your phone, or your private NAS. Provara works perfectly without an internet connection.

If you choose to sync your vault across devices, Provara uses end-to-end encryption. Your data is encrypted with your keys before it ever leaves your device, so even if the sync server is compromised, your memories remain private.

Designed for the 50-Year Horizon

Technology changes fast, but your family's memories shouldn't be trapped in a format that will be obsolete in a decade. Provara uses boring, industry-standard formats (JSON, SHA-256, Ed25519) that are designed to be readable for at least half a century.
